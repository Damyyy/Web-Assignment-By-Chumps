Awards

Due to it's picturesque scenery, the multitude of different cultures and unique island charm, the state of Penang received much attention and love from both the national and international community. Because of this, the state of Penang won many international and national recogision and awards, which are mostly praises for Penang's role as a tourism hotpspot.

List of Awards Won

1) LA Times Ranked Penang as #1 of 16 in Must-Visit Destinations
The Los Angeles Times listed Penang as number 1 in their list of 16 must-visit destinations, mentioning that Penang has beautiful heritage sites, bevy cruise lines and lively food scenes. It also exihibits the true essence of Malaysia, displaying British echoes, Malay culture, Chinese and Indian commercial traditions, and a stew of religions. Indeed a must-visit destination for travelers.
2) The Guardian ranked Georgetown as #8 on it's Holiday Hotspot list
The Guardian listed the state capital of Penang - George Town, as #8 of its top 40 holiday hotspots in 2014. In the article, it said Penangites are renovating everything from Chinese mansions to old factories, which means there’s something for all budgets – delicious street food or fine dining.
3) UNESCO World Heritage Site
A fascinating fusion of eastern and western influences, Penang is Malaysia’s most tourist-visited destination. The island manages to embrace modernity while retaining its colonial traditions; due to its well-preserved heritage buildings Penang’s capital, Georgetown, has been accorded a listing as a UNESCO World Cultural Heritage Site.
4) Penang gained 6 awards from the Hospitality Asia Platinum Awards (HAPA) Malaysia Series

The awards won were dated from 2013 to 2015, and includes:

HAPA Restaurant of the Year – Dining Experience(Hotels/Resorts) – Hard Rock Cafe Penang
HAPA King of Kitchens – The Ambassador of Cuisine – Chef Mohd Khairusshari Bin Ghazali
HAPA 4-Star Hotel/Resort – Exceptional Experience
HAPA Family & Recreation Hotel/Resort – Delightful Experience
HAPA Concept Restaurant – Most Innovative Guest Experience (Hotels/Resorts)
PRE HAPA Public Relations – Excellence
